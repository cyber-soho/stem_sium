To: Academic Leadership

From: Asen Grozdanov, Professor

Date: December 9, 2025

Executive Summary & Rationale

The current assessment model, heavily reliant on computational exams and proctoring, does not align with the practical nature of Integrative Projects.

We propose a Competency-Based Framework shifting focus from isolated testing to Milestone Deliverables.
